About Us


The road will never be the same

Aaho is working towards simplifying inter-city trucking for shippers as well as transporters. Our platform makes it possible to book, track, and manage all your trucking needs in the quickest, simplest and most cost-effective manner.

We strongly believe that relevant technology interventions can make Indian trucking much more efficient and transparent while benefitting all stakeholders. Our platform is an end-to-end solution that works to solve the various problems faced by businesses as well as fleet owners.

Need reliability and efficiency in goods transportation? The answer is Aaho!
