About Aaho

TRUCKING, MEET TECHNOLOGY

Aaho was founded in 2016 with a vision of simplifying long haul trucking by leveraging technology and analytics. Today, hundreds of trucks across the nation move everyday on our platform, delivering enhanced value to shippers, fleet owners and drivers. Our products are designed to solve the age old problems related to sourcing, tracking, payments and asset utilization in trucking, thereby achieving better realizations for businesses as well as fleet owners.

    Why Us?

    Technology at the Core

    No single platform provides such end to end capability as Aaho does. From the time customer submits requirement till delivery and POD upload all shipments are managed digitally.

    TRANSPARENCY

    Shippers face difficulties in finding trucks at the right price, and sometimes at all, due to being restricted to a few trusted suppliers. Aaho taps a wide network of suppliers at the same time via automated processes to ensure speed of sourcing and rationalization of costs.

    EFFICIENCY

    Digitization and automation of documentation, payments and related processes cuts costs and ensures your cargo is not held up for trivial reasons. Shipment monitoring improves driver performance.

    OPTIMIZATION

    We analyze our data constantly to optimize routes, monitor supplier performance and manage operational parameters to improve outcomes for customers and fleet partners.
